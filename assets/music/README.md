# Background music

The player expects these three files (exact names). Drop your MP3s here:

- `my-body-is-a-cage.mp3`   — My Body Is a Cage (Peter Gabriel)
- `a-thousand-years.mp3`    — A Thousand Years (Christina Perri)
- `my-immortal.mp3`         — My Immortal (Evanescence)

Until the files are here, the play button shows a gentle "add the mp3" note.
(These songs are copyrighted — add files you own. We can also switch to a
streaming embed if you'd prefer not to host audio.)
