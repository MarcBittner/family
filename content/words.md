# Words — for Vivienne  (DRAFT — Marc to edit freely)

## Cover
**Vivienne**
There are things I should have said with my whole heart.
So I'm saying them now.

## Chapter 1 — How it started (2015–2016)
It began quietly — you, me, and two cats who were sure the place was theirs.
I didn't know then that these were the best days of my life.
I know it now.

## Chapter 2 — The life we built
We made a home. Ordinary mornings. Road trips. Holidays.
Your hand in mine at the edge of the water.
None of it was loud. All of it was everything.

## Chapter 3 — When we became a family
And then there were three.
The day Augustine arrived, I watched you become a mother —
and I have never loved you more than I did in that moment.

## Augustine ("Boo")
Auggie — your laugh, your whole face lighting up.
Half of it is hers. The best part of me is just trying to be worthy of you.

## Evan
Evan — I didn't get enough days with you yet.
But you are wanted in every single one of them.
I'm becoming the dad you deserve, and I am not going to stop.

## Loki & Filou  (in loving memory)
Loki and Filou — who filled this home, and left it too soon.
Vivienne, you loved them like family, because that's what they were.
They're part of our story too.

## Closing — to Vivienne
I caused you pain. I see it clearly now,
and I won't hide from it or ask you to carry it for me.

Getting sober didn't fix everything — but it gave me my eyes back.
And what I see is you, Auggie, and Evan. The family we made.

I'm staying sober for me, and for our boys — with or without a reason.
But you are the reason I hope for.

Come home. Let me show you — not with words, with every ordinary day —
the man I'm becoming.

I love you. I'm here. I'm not going anywhere.
